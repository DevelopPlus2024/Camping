Popular Campsites:

Below is a list of some popular campgrounds you may want to check out:

1. Camping at Ba Den Mountain in Tay Ninh:

Ba Den Mountain, famous as "The Roof of the South", has become an interesting climbing and camping destination for young people who love to explore1. Ba Den Mountain is located in the northeast of Tay Ninh city, Thanh Tan commune, about 11km1 from the center. Ba Den Mountain has an altitude of 986m above sea level.

The terrain has many large rocks, so it has both a primitive and simple beauty and a high slope, so be careful when moving. For many backpackers and trekkers, this place has a strong attraction because it satisfies the passion for conquering nature.

The most ideal time to camp on Ba Den Mountain is from December to April of the following year. Avoid going from August to November because this is the rainy season in Tay Ninh, not suitable for outdoor travel experiences.

If you want to go to Ba Den Mountain to camp, there are two ways for you to choose: climbing the mountain at an altitude of nearly 1000m or taking the cable car1. The cable car system at Ba Den Mountain has been around for a long time and is always maintained every year, so you can choose to travel by cable car to both save time and energy and enjoy the beautiful scenery outside. below.

Trekking is an extremely attractive choice for those who are passionate about conquering and challenging. Many young people choose Ba Den Mountain as their goal to conquer and camp overnight1. The climbing path is very dangerous, with countless rough rocks, you need to be careful and carefully study the direction.

Before climbing, you need to prepare drinking water and some food.
